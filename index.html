    Job Market Paper

    Let the Market Speak: Using Interest Rates to Identify the Fed Information Effect
    New version coming soon
    Abstract Draft (Nov 12, 2021)

    This paper proposes a novel approach to identifying the Fed information effect and the exogenous monetary shock from an FOMC announcement. The approach brings the market response to macro data releases to an otherwise standard high-frequency identification framework. The model is point-identified, and the decomposition can be done in real time.
